About

Trusted Counsel for
Complex Challenges

Crozier Counsel is a distinctive strategic advisory practice founded by David Crozier CBE, bridging technology, innovation and government to accelerate economic transformation.

With over 25 years operating at the intersection of emerging technology, academia, industry and public policy, the firm delivers authoritative guidance to senior decision-makers facing the defining challenges of the AI era.

Few advisory practices can draw upon the real-time experience of directing a major national AI programme whilst simultaneously advising government on science and innovation policy. That combination of operational depth and strategic perspective is what sets Crozier Counsel apart.

Unlike traditional consultancies, Crozier Counsel operates as a trusted advisor model, providing senior-level strategic counsel rather than large team deployments. Every engagement is led personally by David Crozier, ensuring the depth of insight and quality of counsel that comes only from direct experience at the highest levels.

The firm works with a select number of clients each year across government, academia and industry, specialising in AI adoption strategy, innovation ecosystem development, technology policy and the architecture of complex public-private partnerships.
